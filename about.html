Hi, I'm Jimi

About Me

I'm a researcher and designer who also has two cats, plays D&D, and knows way more about fictional war histories than is probably useful. This page is for the stuff that doesn't fit on a CV.

The cats

I have two cats. Sisu is blind — but you wouldn't know it from watching him. He's memorised the layout of the flat and navigates it entirely from memory, smell, and reflexes. The reflexes are fast. Fast enough that he catches things he shouldn't be able to catch. I've stopped worrying about him. The other one is your standard cat: deeply opinionated, inconsistently affectionate, always sitting somewhere inconvenient.

Lore & games

I play D&D, but more broadly I'm just into fantasy and whatever lore surrounds it. I've spent a lot of time going deep — World of Warcraft, Warhammer 40k, Fallout, D&D setting material. On YouTube, Luetin09 is my go-to: he makes 3+ hour deep dives into Warhammer 40k that I've watched more than once. That should tell you most of what you need to know.

I also follow Dan Carlin's Hardcore History and The Rest Is History podcast. Same impulse, real events. I just like learning about things in a lot of detail.

I'm also working on some creative writing — medieval fantasy, nothing finished, mostly for myself. It's going.

Fitness (and chocolate)

In gymnasium I did a personal trainer and masseur education. I'm not a fitness professional or anything — I just find how the body works interesting. Muscle memory, how training stress leads to adaptation, why recovery matters. It's the kind of thing I read about without any particular reason to.

Chocolate is the other side of this. I used to finish entire bars in one sitting without really thinking about it. I've gotten better — mostly I try to nibble or just not buy it in the first place. It's still a thing I'm working on, but the situation has improved.
