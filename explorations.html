Current interest

Animal-Computer Interaction

I came across ACI not that long ago and it stuck with me. The design problem is genuinely interesting: how do you make technology for users who experience the world completely differently from you, and who can't give you feedback the way a human user can? That's a hard constraint to design around. I'm early in this — reading, thinking. Not much more than that yet.
