Current interest

Animal-Computer Interaction

I came across ACI a while back and it's been sitting in my head since. The basic design problem is similar to what I've worked on before — how do you design for someone who can't tell you what they need? — except the user here isn't human at all. That makes it harder in some ways and more interesting in most ways. I'm early in this: reading papers, watching talks, sketching a few ideas. Nothing to show yet.
